CSV Data Format (for your report)

Each row in pose_hand_data.csv has the following columns:

  • type ‚Äì "pose" for body landmarks, "hand" for hand landmarks.
  • hand_index ‚Äì -1 for pose landmarks; 0 or 1 for each detected hand.
  • landmark_index ‚Äì Index of the landmark (0‚Äì32 for pose, 0‚Äì20 for each hand) following the MediaPipe landmark ordering.
  • timestamp_iso ‚Äì The time when the frame was processed, in ISO-8601 format.
  • x_norm, y_norm, z_norm ‚Äì Normalized coordinates in the camera space (x and y are in [0,1] relative to the image width/height; z is a depth-like value in normalized units).
  • visibility ‚Äì For pose landmarks only: estimated visibility of the landmark (0 to 1). Empty for hand landmarks.
